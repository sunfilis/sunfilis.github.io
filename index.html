About

LUCY (루시) is a four-member band under Mystic Story composed of Shin Yechan (violin), Choi Sangyeop (guitar, vocal), Cho Wonsang (bass, vocal) and Shin Gwangil (drum, vocal). Formed through the survival show JTBC Superband, they made their debut on May 8, 2020 with their first single "Dear.". "I Got U" is the title track of their fourth single album <Gatcha!>.
